零状态RLC串联电路的自然响应

  • 拓扑约束
    • KVL: uC=uL+uRu_C=u_L+u_RuC​=uL​+uR​
    • KCL: iC=iL=iR=ii_C=i_L=i_R=iiC​=iL​=iR​=i
  • 元件约束
    • iC=CduCdti_C=C\frac{du_C}{dt}iC​=CdtduC​​
    • uL=LdiLdtu_L=L\frac{di_L}{dt}uL​=LdtdiL​​
    • R=uRiRR=\frac{u_R}{i_R}R=iR​uR​​

列写方程：

uL+uR=uCLddtiL+RiR=uCLddt(iC)+R(iC)=uCLddt(CduCdt)+R(CduCdt)=uCLCd2uCd2t+RCduCdt=uCLCd2uCd2t+RCduCdt−uC=0d2uCd2t+RLduCdt−1LCuC=0 \begin{align*} u_L+u_R&=u_C \\ L\frac{d}{dt}i_L+R i_R&=u_C \\ L\frac{d}{dt}(i_C)+R (i_C)&=u_C \\ L\frac{d}{dt}(C\frac{du_C}{dt})+R(C\frac{du_C}{dt})&=u_C \\ LC\frac{d^2u_C}{d^2t}+RC\frac{du_C}{dt}&=u_C \\ LC\frac{d^2u_C}{d^2t}+RC\frac{du_C}{dt}-u_C&=0 \\ \frac{d^2u_C}{d^2t}+\frac{R}{L}\frac{du_C}{dt}-\frac{1}{LC}u_C&=0 \\ \end{align*} uL​+uR​Ldtd​iL​+RiR​Ldtd​(iC​)+R(iC​)Ldtd​(CdtduC​​)+R(CdtduC​​)LCd2td2uC​​+RCdtduC​​LCd2td2uC​​+RCdtduC​​−uC​d2td2uC​​+LR​dtduC​​−LC1​uC​​=uC​=uC​=uC​=uC​=uC​=0=0​

定义：2α=RL2\alpha=\frac{R}{L}2α=LR​，ω02=1LC\omega_0^2=\frac{1}{LC}ω02​=LC1​，得：

d2uCd2t+2αduCdt−ω02uC=0 \frac{d^2u_C}{d^2t}+2\alpha\frac{du_C}{dt}-\omega_0^2u_C=0 \\ d2td2uC​​+2αdtduC​​−ω02​uC​=0

特征方程为

λ2+2αλ−ω02=0 \lambda^2+2\alpha\lambda-\omega_0^2=0 λ2+2αλ−ω02​=0

判别式为

Δ=b2−4ac=(2α)2−4×1×(ω02)=4α2−4ω02=4(α2−ω02) \begin{align*} \Delta&=b^2-4ac\\ &=(2\alpha)^2-4 \times 1 \times (\omega_0^2)\\ &=4\alpha^2-4\omega_0^2 \\ &=4(\alpha^2-\omega_0^2) \\ \end{align*} Δ​=b2−4ac=(2α)2−4×1×(ω02​)=4α2−4ω02​=4(α2−ω02​)​

特征根为

λ=−b±Δ2a=−(2α)±4(α2−ω02)2×1=−2α±2α2−ω022×1=−α±α2−ω02 \begin{align*} \lambda &= \frac{-b\pm\sqrt{\Delta}}{2a} \\ &= \frac{-(2\alpha) \pm \sqrt{4(\alpha^2-\omega_0^2)}}{2\times 1} \\ &= \frac{-2\alpha \pm 2\sqrt{\alpha^2-\omega_0^2}}{2\times 1} \\ &= -\alpha \pm \sqrt{\alpha^2-\omega_0^2} \\ \end{align*} λ​=2a−b±Δ​​=2×1−(2α)±4(α2−ω02​)​​=2×1−2α±2α2−ω02​​​=−α±α2−ω02​​​

根据判别式的情况，微分方程的解为：

过阻尼情况： 当α2>ω02当 \alpha^2>\omega_0^2当α2>ω02​ ( 即 Δ=4(α2−ω02)>0\Delta=4(\alpha^2-\omega_0^2)>0Δ=4(α2−ω02​)>0 ) 时

λ1,λ2=−α±α2−ω02uC=Aeλ1t+Beλ2t \begin{align*} \lambda_1,\lambda_2 &= -\alpha \pm \sqrt{\alpha^2-\omega_0^2} \\ u_C&=Ae^{\lambda_1t}+Be^{\lambda_2t} \end{align*} λ1​,λ2​uC​​=−α±α2−ω02​​=Aeλ1​t+Beλ2​t​

临界阻尼情况： 当α2=ω02\alpha^2=\omega_0^2α2=ω02​ ( 即 Δ=4(α2−ω02)=0\Delta=4(\alpha^2-\omega_0^2)=0Δ=4(α2−ω02​)=0 ) 时

λ=−α±α2−ω02=−α±0=−αuC=Aeλt+Bteλt \begin{align*} \lambda &= -\alpha \pm \sqrt{\alpha^2-\omega_0^2} \\ &= -\alpha \pm 0 \\ &= -\alpha \\ u_C&=Ae^{\lambda t}+Bte^{\lambda t} \end{align*} λuC​​=−α±α2−ω02​​=−α±0=−α=Aeλt+Bteλt​

欠阻尼情况： 当α2<ω02当 \alpha^2<\omega_0^2当α2<ω02​ ( 即 Δ=4(α2−ω02)<0\Delta=4(\alpha^2-\omega_0^2)<0Δ=4(α2−ω02​)<0 ) 时

λ1,λ2=−α±α2−ω02=−α±(−1)×(ω02−α2)=−α±−1×(ω02−α2)=−α±i×(ω02−α2)ωd=(ω02−α2)uC=e−αt(Acos⁡(ωdt)+Bsin⁡(ωdt))R=A2+B2ϕ=tan⁡−1ABuC=Re−αtsin⁡(ωdt+ϕ) \begin{align*} \lambda_1,\lambda_2 &= -\alpha \pm \sqrt{\alpha^2-\omega_0^2} \\ &= -\alpha \pm \sqrt{(-1) \times (\omega_0^2-\alpha^2)} \\ &= -\alpha \pm \sqrt{-1} \times \sqrt{(\omega_0^2-\alpha^2)} \\ &= -\alpha \pm i \times \sqrt{(\omega_0^2-\alpha^2)} \\ \\ \omega_d&=\sqrt{(\omega_0^2-\alpha^2)} \\ u_C&=e^{-\alpha t} ( A \cos(\omega_d t) + B\sin(\omega_d t) ) \\ \\ R &= \sqrt{A^2 + B^2} \\ \phi &= \tan^{-1}\frac{A}{B} \\ u_C &= R e^{-\alpha t} \sin(\omega_d t + \phi) \\ \end{align*} λ1​,λ2​ωd​uC​RϕuC​​=−α±α2−ω02​​=−α±(−1)×(ω02​−α2)​=−α±−1​×(ω02​−α2)​=−α±i×(ω02​−α2)​=(ω02​−α2)​=e−αt(Acos(ωd​t)+Bsin(ωd​t))=A2+B2​=tan−1BA​=Re−αtsin(ωd​t+ϕ)​

零阻尼情况： 当α=0当 \alpha=0当α=0 ( 即 Δ=4(α2−ω02)=−4ω02<0\Delta=4(\alpha^2-\omega_0^2)=-4\omega_0^2<0Δ=4(α2−ω02​)=−4ω02​<0 ) 时

λ1,λ2=−α±α2−ω02=−0±02−ω02=±−1×ω02=±−1×ω02=±iω02(特征根为纯虚数)ωd=ω02=ω0uC=e−αt(Acos⁡(ωdt)+Bsin⁡(ωdt))=e−0t(Acos⁡(ωdt)+Bsin⁡(ωdt))=Acos⁡(ωdt)+Bsin⁡(ωdt)=Acos⁡(ω0t)+Bsin⁡(ω0t)R=A2+B2θ=tan⁡−1ABuC=Re−αtsin⁡(ωdt+θ)=Re−0tsin⁡(ωdt+θ)=Rsin⁡(ωdt+θ)=Rsin⁡(ω0t+θ) \begin{align*} \lambda_1,\lambda_2 &= -\alpha \pm \sqrt{\alpha^2-\omega_0^2} \\ &= -0 \pm \sqrt{0^2-\omega_0^2} \\ &= \pm \sqrt{-1 \times \omega_0^2} \\ &= \pm \sqrt{-1} \times \sqrt{\omega_0^2} \\ &= \pm i\sqrt{\omega_0^2} \\ &(\text{特征根为纯虚数}) \\ \\ \omega_d&=\sqrt{\omega_0^2}=\omega_0 \\ u_C&=e^{-\alpha t} ( A \cos(\omega_d t) + B\sin(\omega_d t) ) \\ &=e^{-0 t} ( A \cos(\omega_d t) + B\sin(\omega_d t) ) \\ &=A \cos(\omega_d t) + B\sin(\omega_d t) \\ &=A \cos(\omega_0 t) + B\sin(\omega_0 t) \\ \\ R &= \sqrt{A^2 + B^2} \\ \theta &= \tan^{-1}\frac{A}{B} \\ u_C &= R e^{-\alpha t} \sin(\omega_d t + \theta) \\ &= R e^{-0 t} \sin(\omega_d t + \theta) \\ &= R \sin(\omega_d t + \theta) \\ &= R \sin(\omega_0 t + \theta) \\ \end{align*} λ1​,λ2​ωd​uC​RθuC​​=−α±α2−ω02​​=−0±02−ω02​​=±−1×ω02​​=±−1​×ω02​​=±iω02​​(特征根为纯虚数)=ω02​​=ω0​=e−αt(Acos(ωd​t)+Bsin(ωd​t))=e−0t(Acos(ωd​t)+Bsin(ωd​t))=Acos(ωd​t)+Bsin(ωd​t)=Acos(ω0​t)+Bsin(ω0​t)=A2+B2​=tan−1BA​=Re−αtsin(ωd​t+θ)=Re−0tsin(ωd​t+θ)=Rsin(ωd​t+θ)=Rsin(ω0​t+θ)​
